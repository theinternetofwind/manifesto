The Internet of Wind Manifesto initiators

The first draft of the Internet of wind manifesto (version 0.1) has been written by a group of young professionals from southern Italy, listed here under.

If you are reading this document is because you inspired us and helped us to be here. We asked your contribution and we look forward to have your face here together with ours.

Please contribute also to the text below:

We want to live in a better world and every day we work to build it.

Francesco Paraggio, Environmental Engineer, WindStack IVS

Who inspired us

Many movements, people and thought leaders have inspired us.

Here an incomplete and unordered list:

Stromrebellen at EWS, Paul Gipe at Wind-Works, Richard Stallman at Free software foundation, Hummers at IETF, Javier Ruiz Diaz at Open Rights Group, Jane Dennett Torpe at Onawi, Rufus Pollok at Open Knowledge Foundation, Preben Maegaard at Nordic Folkcenter, Hans Rosling at GapMinder, Annie Leonard at The Story of Stuff

This manifesto is meant as a collaborative work. We have initiated this process, now is your turn!
